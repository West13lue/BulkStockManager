Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CloudStore ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you install and use our Shopify application Bulk Stock Manager.

1. Information We Collect

When you install and use Bulk Stock Manager, we collect the following information:

1.1 Information from Shopify

  • Store information: Store name, domain, email address, and timezone
  • Product data: Product titles, SKUs, variants, inventory levels, and prices
  • Location data: Inventory locations configured in your store
  • Order data: Order information for inventory tracking and analytics (read-only)

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Supplier information (names, contact details, terms)
  • Batch and lot tracking data
  • Purchase orders and inventory counts
  • User preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bulk Stock Manager application
  • Synchronize inventory data with your Shopify store
  • Generate analytics, reports, and forecasts
  • Send notifications about low stock or expiring products
  • Improve and optimize our services
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Data is stored on secure servers with regular backups.

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed
  • We do not store sensitive payment information
  • Data is hosted in secure data centers

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With Shopify: To sync inventory and fulfill app functionality
  • Service providers: Hosting and infrastructure services that help us operate the app
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your app remains installed. When you uninstall the app:

  • Your access tokens are immediately revoked
  • Your data is scheduled for deletion within 30 days
  • You may request immediate deletion by contacting us

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our app uses essential cookies only for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering your preferences

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
